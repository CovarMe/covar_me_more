Welcome to CovarMe

As part of the 2016/17 cohort of the Data Science course at the Barcelona Graduate School of Economics, we have build this application to implement some of the tools and techniques gained during the course and to showcase our skills in data-processing and computing.


This application lets you select a bunch of stock tickers and a desired time horizon. Then, the application uses predicted covariance over the time horizon to allocate the portfolio weights using dynamic programming.
